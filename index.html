Our Goals for this Website

This website explores the role of implicit bias and intersectionality in technology and digital spaces. In today's world, technological development is not just about innovation but also about inclusivity, ethics, and the impact of systemic bias on different communities. Understanding these factors helps create more equitable systems and ensures that technology serves all individuals fairly.


This website aims to bridge the gap between technical innovation and social awareness by examining the ethical dimensions of digital development. Whether you're a student, developer, or researcher, we invite you to explore these critical topics and contribute to the ongoing conversation about fairness, inclusion, and the future of technology.
