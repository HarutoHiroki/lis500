Brittany Packnett Cunningham

Discover More

Tech Hero

Brittany Packnett Cunningham is a leader in tech and social justice, an advocate for advancing racial equity within the tech industry. She is a passionate speaker, writer, and educator who uses her platform to challenge the status quo and create systems of equity. As the co-founder of TechBridge, Brittany provides mentorship to young people of color entering the tech field, empowering them to not only succeed but challenge and redefine the future of tech.

Brittany is also known for her role in the TEDx platform, where she highlights the intersectionality of race, class, and technology. She advocates for inclusive practices and policies that ensure people from marginalized communities, particularly Black and Latinx groups, are represented in tech development.

In addition to her advocacy work, Brittany has collaborated with People of Color in Tech to build networks of support and knowledge sharing within communities that have been historically underrepresented in tech.
